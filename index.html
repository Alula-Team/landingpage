Here is an overview of Alula.

  • Export reports for easy tax filings
  • Digitally create leases
  • Manage properties on any device
  • Add properties as you acquire them. No caps or minimums

Whether you are using the mobile app or website, Alula is easy to use. Sign up, link your bank account to accept rent payments, add properties, add tenants and add transactions. We keep track of everything, so when you are ready to file taxes, just export your pdf reportings.

No catch! With Alula, it doesn't matter if you have one property or one million properties. We do not place a cap on properties added and there are no monthly pricing minimums.

Alula is free for landlords. Tenants do pay a 1% transaction fee when paying their rent. This is still cheaper than most of the other property management sites out there.

Payments are transfered from the Tenant's bank account to the Landlord's bank account, making it easy to collect rent and avoiding trips to the bank. Both parties just have to connect their accounts. We use Dwolla, one of the best and most secure payment processors out there.
